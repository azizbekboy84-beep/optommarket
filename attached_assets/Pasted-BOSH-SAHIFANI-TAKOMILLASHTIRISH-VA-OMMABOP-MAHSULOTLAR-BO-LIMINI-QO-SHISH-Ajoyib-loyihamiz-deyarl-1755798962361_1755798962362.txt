Aniq qadamlar:

1-QISM: BACKENDNI TAYYORLASH

Baza Sxemasini Yangilash (shared/schema.ts):

products jadvaliga yangi is_featured (boolean, standart qiymati false) nomli ustun qo'shing. Bu ustun qaysi mahsulotlarni bosh sahifada "ommabop" sifatida ko'rsatishni belgilash uchun ishlatiladi.

Yangi API Endpoint Yaratish:

GET /api/products/featured nomli yangi endpoint yarating.

Bu endpoint bazadagi is_featured belgisi true bo'lgan barcha mahsulotlarni (masalan, 8-10 ta) qaytarsin.

Admin Panelini Yangilash:

AdminProductsPage.tsx sahifasidagi mahsulot qo'shish/tahrirlash formasiga "Bosh sahifada ko'rsatish" (is_featured) uchun yangi Checkbox qo'shing.

/api/admin/products endpoint'larini bu yangi is_featured ma'lumotini qabul qiladigan qilib yangilang.

2-QISM: FRONTENDNI QAYTA ISHLASH

Bosh Sahifani Tozalash (HomePage.tsx):

"Bizning afzalliklarimiz" va "Nega Aynan Bizni Tanlash Kerak?" bo'limlaridan birini to'liq o'chirib tashlang.

"Ommabop Mahsulotlar" Bo'limini Yaratish:

O'chirilgan bo'lim o'rniga "Ommabop Mahsulotlar" sarlavhasi bilan yangi bo'lim yarating.

TanStack Query yordamida yangi GET /api/products/featured endpoint'idan ma'lumotlarni oling.

Olingan mahsulotlarni ProductCard.tsx komponenti yordamida chiroyli gorizontal karusel (carousel) yoki grid shaklida ko'rsating.
